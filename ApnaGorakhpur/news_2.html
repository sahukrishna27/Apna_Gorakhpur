CM Yogi launches 42 E-buses in Gorakhpur, a step towards clean and green transportation

42 Electric buses launched in gorakhpur, images surfaces

Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on Wednesday flagged off 42 electric buses in Gorakhpur. The buses will be operated by the Gorakhpur City Transport Services Limited

Speaking at the event, CM Yogi said that the introduction of electric buses is a major step towards making Gorakhpur a pollution-free city. He said that the buses will help to reduce air pollution and noise pollution in the city.

He also said that the buses will provide a comfortable and affordable mode of transportation for the citizens of Gorakhpur. The buses are equipped with all modern facilities, including CCTV cameras, GPS tracking system, and panic buttons. They have a seating capacity of 35 passengers.
