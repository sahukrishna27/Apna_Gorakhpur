Rail Museum

Introduction

The Railway Museum in Gorakhpur is a must-visit for anyone interested in the history of Indian railways. The museum is home to a wide variety of exhibits, including old locomotives, coaches, and signaling equipment. Visitors can also learn about the history of the Indian Railways through interactive displays and multimedia presentations.

History

The Railway Museum in Gorakhpur was established in 2005. The museum is housed in a 120-year-old building that was originally used as a residence for British railway officials. The museum's collection includes over 100 exhibits, dating from the early days of Indian railways

Exhibits

The exhibits at the Railway Museum in Gorakhpur are divided into several sections, including:

Locomotives: The museum has a large collection of old locomotives, including the first locomotive used by the North Indian Railway.

Coaches: The museum also has a collection of old coaches, including first-class saloons and dining cars.

Signaling equipment: The museum has a collection of signaling equipment, including semaphores, bells, and whistles.

Interactive displays: The museum has several interactive displays that teach visitors about the history of Indian railways.

Multimedia presentations: The museum also has several multimedia presentations that tell the story of Indian railways.

Toy Train

One of the most popular attractions at the Railway Museum in Gorakhpur is the toy train. The toy train is a miniature steam locomotive that takes visitors on a 1.5 km ride around the museum grounds.

Conclusion

The Railway Museum in Gorakhpur is a fascinating place to learn about the history of Indian railways. The museum's collection of exhibits is extensive and informative, and the interactive displays and multimedia presentations are a great way to engage visitors of all ages. If you're interested in railways, or if you're just looking for a fun and educational day out, the Railway Museum in Gorakhpur is definitely worth a visit.
