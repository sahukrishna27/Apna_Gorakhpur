Gorakhnath Temple 8

Introduction

Gorakhpur is located in the eastern part of Uttar Pradesh, on the banks of the Rapti River. The city is surrounded by the districts of Deoria, Kushinagar, Siddharthnagar, and Maharajganj.

Gorakhpur is well connected with all major cities of India.

History

The temple is said to have been founded by Gorakhnath, a 10th-century saint who was one of the most important figures in the Nath tradition. Gorakhnath was a master of Hatha Yoga, and the temple is a major center for the practice of yoga
