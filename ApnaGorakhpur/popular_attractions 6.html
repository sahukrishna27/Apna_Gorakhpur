MMMUT

Introduction

Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) is a premier state technical university in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 2013 by upgrading Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, which was established in 1962, into a university. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, technology, applied sciences, and management. It is also home to a number of research centers and institutes.

History

The history of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology can be traced back to 1962, when it was established as Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College. The college was named after Madan Mohan Malaviya, a prominent Indian independence leader and educationist. In 2013, the college was upgraded into a university by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. The university was renamed Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology.

Vision and Mission

The vision of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology is to be a leading university in the field of engineering, technology, and management. The mission of the university is to provide high-quality education and research, and to create globally competitive graduates

Academics

MMMUT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, technology, applied sciences, and management. The university has a number of well-equipped laboratories and workshops, and it also has a library with a large collection of books and journals.

Facilities

MMMUT has a well-equipped campus with state-of-the-art facilities. The university has a library, a computer center, a sports complex, and a hostel facility. It also has a number of research centers and laboratories.

Conclusion

Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology is a premier state technical university in India. The university offers a wide range of academic programs and has a strong focus on research. MMMUT has been recognized by a number of organizations for its excellence in education and research.
