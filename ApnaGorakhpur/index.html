Since Yogi Adityanath became the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh in 2017, Gorakhpur has undergone a major transformation.

The city has seen a significant improvement in its infrastructure, with new roads, schools, and hospitals being built. The law and order situation has also improved, making Gorakhpur a safer place to live.

In addition, the city has become a major tourist destination.

Under the leadership of Yogi Adityanath', the city has become a much better place to live.
