How to reach?

Introduction

Gorakhpur is located in the eastern part of Uttar Pradesh, on the banks of the Rapti River. The city is surrounded by the districts of Deoria, Kushinagar, Siddharthnagar, and Maharajganj.

Gorakhpur is well connected with all major cities of India.

Roadways

By Airways

Gorakhpur Airport is the nearest airport, located 8 kilometers from the city center. There are direct flights from Gorakhpur airport to Delhi, Varanasi, Lucknow, Kolkata, and Mumbai.

Other airports near Gorakhpur are Varanasi Airport (235 kilometers), Lucknow Airport (276 kilometers), and Allahabad Airport (339 kilometers).

By Train

Gorakhpur Junction is the main railway station in Gorakhpur. It is well connected to all major cities in India. Other major railway stations near Gorakhpur are Faizabad Junction (121 kilometers), Kushinagar Junction (50 kilometers), and Basti Junction (100 kilometers).

Other airports near Gorakhpur are Varanasi Airport (235 kilometers), Lucknow Airport (276 kilometers), and Allahabad Airport (339 kilometers).
