Nauka Vihar

Introduction

Nauka Vihar is a beautiful lake in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. It is a popular tourist destination, especially during the summer months. The lake is surrounded by lush green trees and gardens, and there are several boating facilities available. There is also a Buddha Museum, a planetarium, and a park on the premises. Nauka Vihar is a beautiful and relaxing place to visit, and a great place to spend a day with family and friends.

History

Nauka Vihar was built in the 1970s by the then Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh, Kamalapati Tripathi. The lake was originally called Ramgarh Tal, but it was renamed Nauka Vihar in the 1980s.

Attractions

The main attraction of Nauka Vihar is the boating facilities. There are several types of boats available, including paddle boats, row boats, and speed boats. You can also go for a swim in the lake, or just relax on the banks and enjoy the scenery.

Other attractions at Nauka Vihar includes Buddha museum and Planetarium

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Nauka Vihar is during the summer months (April to June). However, the lake is also a popular destination during the winter months (November to February).

How to Reach

Nauka Vihar is located in the Ramgarh area of Gorakhpur. It is about 5 kilometers from the Gorakhpur Railway Station. You can reach Nauka Vihar by rickshaw, taxi, or bus.
